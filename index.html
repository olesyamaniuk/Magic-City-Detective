About

Magic City Detective: Rage is an action-packed detective adventure game where players assume the role of a sharp-witted investigator with magical abilities. Set in a sprawling, mystical city full of dark secrets, players must use their detective skills and supernatural powers to solve challenging cases. The world is filled with intrigue, danger, and hidden magic that only the bravest can uncover.

Explore enchanted environments, interact with magical creatures, and piece together clues to crack the most complex cases the city has ever seen. Are you ready to face the wrath of powerful foes while unraveling the greatest mysteries?
